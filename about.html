About Dev-Maze

Hello! My name is Nathan Russo, and I am thrilled you are checking out my game.

I have always enjoyed making mazes. I would draw massive ones in the sand at the beach. During my freshmen year, I participated in CSH Hacks. During that event, I decided to generate version 0 of Dev-Maze. Version 0 had no name and was written in Python, using Turtle. It generated a maze and allowed the player (turtle) to move from the start corner to the end corner within the borders.
